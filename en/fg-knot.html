Fishing Knots / FG Knot

FG Knot

Difficulty: Hard Strength: Very High Purpose: Braid-to-leader

How to Tie the FG Knot

  1. Keep your braid under tension (use a rod or hold with your teeth).
  2. Lay the leader over the braid at an angle.
  3. Weave the braid over and under the leader alternately, 15-20 times.
  4. Secure with 2-3 half hitches pulled tight against the wraps.
  5. Trim the leader tag flush.
  6. Add 3-4 more half hitches moving away from the wraps.
  7. Trim the braid tag and apply a small drop of UV knot glue if desired.

Pros

  • Strongest braid-to-leader knot (near 100% strength)
  • Ultra-slim profile flows through guides without catching
  • No bulk — ideal for long casting

Cons

  • Difficult to learn and requires practice
  • Needs tension on the braid while tying
  • Time-consuming compared to simpler alternatives

Best Used For

Connecting braided mainline to fluorocarbon or monofilament leader. The top choice for serious saltwater and inshore anglers.
