Arbor Knot

Difficulty: Easy Strength: Medium Purpose: Reel

How to Tie the Arbor Knot

  1. Wrap the line around the reel spool.
  2. Tie an overhand knot around the standing line.
  3. Tie a second overhand knot in the tag end (this acts as a stopper).
  4. Pull the standing line so the first knot slides down and the stopper knot jams against it.
  5. Trim the tag end.

Pros

  • Extremely simple to tie
  • Works with all line types and reel types
  • Only needs to hold while initial line is wound on

Cons

  • Not strong enough to fight fish directly from the arbor
  • Can slip on very smooth spool surfaces

Best Used For

Attaching any fishing line to a spinning, baitcasting, or fly reel spool. The starting point of every reel setup.
