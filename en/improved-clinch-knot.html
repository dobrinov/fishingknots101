Improved Clinch Knot

Difficulty: Easy Strength: High Purpose: Hook-to-line

How to Tie the Improved Clinch Knot

  1. Thread the line through the hook eye and pull about 15 cm through.
  2. Wrap the tag end around the standing line 5-7 times.
  3. Thread the tag end through the small loop near the hook eye.
  4. Then pass it through the large loop you just created.
  5. Moisten and pull tight. Trim the tag end.

Pros

  • Very easy to learn — ideal for beginners
  • Works well with monofilament and fluorocarbon
  • Consistent knot strength around 90%

Cons

  • Not recommended for braided line
  • May slip with lines over 30 lb test

Best Used For

Tying hooks, lures, and swivels to monofilament or fluorocarbon line up to 30 lb test. The go-to knot for freshwater fishing.
